To support Indian speaker travel abroad and deliver their talk we are planning to support. PSSI will support total combined budget limit of 1.2 Lakh INR for international trips per year.

Below are guidelines

  1. Financial Aid can avail only once in lifetime.
  2. His/Her Talk should be selected.
  3. It will be mostly on First Come First Serve but preference will be given to the people applying for the first time.
  4. The supported amount per person with a maximum of Rs. 30,000.
  5. Amount will be in be deposited to person's bank account.
